  • Cinema 21: Local movie theater showing new and old movies! Bonus! They serve Escape from New York Pizza, another local favorite.
  • McMenamins Bagdad Theater & Pub: Shows limited selections of movies along with a full Mcmenamins restaurant menu.
  • Leif Erikson, Bikeable Trail: 11 mile-long trail for biking, hiking, and even horseback riding. Provides a look into Forest Park and the outdoors in Portland, OR for someone with any level of hiking experience.
  • Kells Irish Restaurant & Pub: Iconic bar and restaurant serving traditional Irish food and drinks!
  • McMenamins Crystal Ballroom: Local venue hosting big and small names alike. Famous for it's moving dancefloor!
  • Revolution Hall: Music venue in an old high school! Hosting big and local bands.
  • Providence Park: Home of the Portland Timbers and Thorns, the park held it's first concert series in 20 years in the Summer of 2024. They have hosted the Foo Fighters and will be hosting Post Malone in 2025.

About the Map

Portland is home to some beautiful places and buildings. This map provides a brief introduction to some of the locally well-known spots for entertainment and activities to pass the time in the Rose City.

In this list are theaters, a bar, a place to bike, and music venues in Portland. Each offers something unique, from a pint of Guiness at Kells to a scenic bike ride through Forest Park.

Enjoy the Rose City and all it has to offer!
